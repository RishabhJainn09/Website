Meet Dr. Satish Sanil

With over 20 years of experience in dentistry, Dr. Satish Sanil has become one of Mumbai's most trusted dental practitioners. Known for his conservative approach and patient-first philosophy, Dr. Sanil believes in providing only the necessary treatments without recommending unnecessary procedures.

Located in the heart of Andheri West, our clinic combines modern dental technology with compassionate care. Dr. Sanil's expertise spans general dentistry, cosmetic procedures, and complex dental surgeries, all delivered with precision and care.

Our patients appreciate Dr. Sanil's gentle demeanor, thorough explanations, and commitment to long-lasting results. Many treatments performed over two decades ago continue to serve patients well today.
